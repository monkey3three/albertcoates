Albert Coates

Early Life WW1 Source Code WW2 Burma-Thailand Railway Post War

Albert Coates was a incredible person who served in World War 1, World War 2, helped people when he was a POW building a railway near Burma (now called Myanmar) and Thailand. That is just some of the amazing things Albert Coates did. This website will tell you all about Albert Coates. Click on the buttons to use the website and learn all about him and what he did! It is coded using HTML5, CSS, and Javascript, and deployed using Github.
